Book John Thompson

John’s keynote, I Can. I Will., is a raw, funny, powerful message about limb loss, survival, faith, family, recovery, phantom pain, prosthetics, and finding purpose after life changes forever.

John does not deliver empty motivational clichés. He delivers a message earned through experience that challenges people to stop focusing only on what they lost and start discovering what is still possible.

“I Can. I Will.”

Because the only word that does not belong in your vocabulary is can’t.
